About the Designer


Courtney Sciarrillo is a Software Developer at WeCanCodeIT, a full-time 5-star rated coding bootcamp which teaches agile full-stack software development. She is currently enrolled in the Summer 2018 cohort with an expected graduation date of August 2018.


Prior to enrolling at WCCI, Courtney spent over four years in the Customer Service/Inside Sales industry. With a lifelong interest in both technology and design, Courtney decided to pursue a career which combines both areas of interest.


After graduation, she hopes to focus her career in Front End Development + Web Design. Additionally, Courtney would like to further her career in Graphic Design.
